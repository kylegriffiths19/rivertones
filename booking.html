Bookings

Concerts, special birthdays, Anniversaries

Prices range between £150 -£300 depending on requirements for a variety of events such as fund raising concerts, special birthdays, anniversaries and weddings, in fact any occasion where you would like the 'something special' factor!

We sing because we enjoy it and any income received pays for the licensing and copyright of our music and for items of uniform.

Weddings

When you consider our charges, they compare very very favourably with other items available to enhance a couple's perfect wedding day - in fact it is a very inexpensive add-on facility for any small or large wedding. Young and Old - don't forget your mum, dad, nan and grandad, aunties and uncles – they all will enjoy our very different performances. Whether it is just for the arrival of your guests as they chat and have a drink or for a more formal singing appearance at the end of the meal – or in fact both – you'll be surprised how inexpensive our packages are.
